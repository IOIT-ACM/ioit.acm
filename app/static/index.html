Day 01

Opening Ceremony & Preliminary Debates

Saturday, 14 March 2025

REPORTING & BREAKFAST
8:30 – 9:30
3rd Floor Balcony

delegates arrive, sign in, and enjoy morning refreshments before the day's proceedings begin.

OPENING CEREMONY
9:30 – 11:00
MPH 2nd Floor

inaugural address by the Secretary-General, lighting of the lamp, and cultural performances setting the tone for two days of democratic discourse.

COMMITTEE SESSION 1
11:00 – 1:30
AIPPM: 016 MLA: 216

delegates present position papers and engage in moderated caucuses on their assigned agendas. Rules of procedure strictly observed.

LUNCH BREAK
1:30 – 2:30
3rd Floor Balcony

informal networking. Delegates, chairs, and faculty advisors mingle over a relaxed afternoon meal.

COMMITTEE SESSION 2
2:30 – 4:30
AIPPM: 016 MLA: 216

unmoderated caucus and draft resolution writing. Alliances form, amendments are proposed, and bloc positions begin to take shape.

COMMITTEE PHOTOGRAPH
4:30 – 5:00
Shahu Maharaj Statue

all delegates and chairs gather for the official group photo session.

HIGH TEA
5:00 – 5:30
3rd Floor Balcony

a relaxed evening refreshment break to recharge before the social evening begins.

SOCIALS
5:30 – 8:00
MPH 2nd Floor

evening mixer with games, music, and interactions. An opportunity for delegates to unwind and build bonds across committees.

Day 02

Final Sessions & Closing Ceremony

Sunday, 15 March 2025

BREAKFAST & REPORTING
9:00 – 10:00
3rd Floor Balcony

delegates arrive for Day 2, sign in, and enjoy morning breakfast before sessions resume.

COMMITTEE SESSION 3
10:00 – 1:00
AIPPM: 016 MLA: 216

intensive debate continues. Delegates finalise draft resolutions, merge working papers, and lock bloc positions ahead of the voting procedure.

LUNCH
1:00 – 2:00
3rd Floor Balcony

delegates recharge over a meal before the final afternoon sessions and closing proceedings.

COMMITTEE SESSION 4
2:00 – 4:00
AIPPM: 016 MLA: 216

final lobbying round and formal voting procedure. Roll call, voting on all draft resolutions, and passing of the final communiqué.

HIGH TEA
4:00 – 4:30
3rd Floor Balcony

a refreshment break as the event winds down and delegates prepare for the grand closing ceremony.

CLOSING CEREMONY
4:30 – 6:00
MPH 2nd Floor

Awards — Best Delegate, Outstanding Delegate, and Verbal Mention awards presented. Chief Guest address followed by group photograph and vote of thanks.
