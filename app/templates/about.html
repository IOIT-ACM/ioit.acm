What is ACM?

As a proud student chapter of the international Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), the AISSMS IOIT ACM Student Chapter is a vibrant and inclusive community at AISSMS Institute of Information Technology. As one of the premier technology-focused organizations in the region, we welcome students from all disciplines and skill levels to collaborate, innovate, and share our passion for technology.

Through a diverse range of initiatives, including flagship events like TENET 2024, IOIT MUN, Tech SIGs, and our monthly coding contest Bit by Bit, we aim to inspire and engage students in cutting-edge domains such as artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, virtual reality, web and mobile development, and more. With workshops, speaker panels, hackathons, and competitions, our chapter fosters learning, creativity, and leadership, creating opportunities for all to thrive in the ever-evolving tech landscape.

What we do?

An ACM Student Chapter, affiliated with the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), primarily focuses on providing a platform for computer science students to network, learn, and develop their skills through organizing technical talks, workshops, coding competitions, and other events, essentially acting as a hub for connecting students with industry professionals and fostering a vibrant computing community within their university.

Key Functions of an ACM Student Chapter

  • Educational events: Hosting guest lectures, seminars, and workshops on current trends in computing by industry experts.
  • Competitions: Organizing coding competitions and hackathons to encourage problem-solving and practical application of skills.
  • Mentorship opportunities: Connecting students with experienced professionals for guidance and career advice.
  • Networking events: Facilitating interaction among students, faculty, and industry professionals through social gatherings and meetups.
  • Community outreach: Participating in local tech events, volunteering activities, and promoting computer science education.
  • Professional development: Providing resources for career preparation like resume workshops and interview skills training.
