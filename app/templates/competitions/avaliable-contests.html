Bit by Bit

Bit by Bit is the traditional ACM IOIT competition conducted every month. It features challenging algorithmic problems with exciting prizes for the top performers. This is your chance to compete with the best minds in competitive programming.

Bit by Query

Bit by Query is a DBMS-themed contest focusing on SQL challenges. Test your skills in crafting queries, optimizing database operations, and solving real-world problems through SQL.

Bit by Keystroke

Bit by Keystroke is a competitive typing challenge that tests speed, accuracy, and mental focus through code typing, error correction, and memory-based rounds.The competition emphasizes precision and problem-solving across structured technical tasks.

Bit by Design

Bit by Design is a high-energy online design competition that challenges participants to think fast, design smart, and push their creative limits. Within a limited time, designers respond to unique prompts and transform ideas into compelling web experiences - testing creativity, clarity, and problem-solving under pressure.
