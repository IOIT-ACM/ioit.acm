Bit by Bit

Bit by Bit is the traditional ACM IOIT competition conducted every month. It features challenging algorithmic problems with exciting prizes for the top performers. This is your chance to compete with the best minds in competitive programming.

Bit by Query

Bit by Query is a DBMS-themed contest focusing on SQL challenges. Test your skills in crafting queries, optimizing database operations, and solving real-world problems through SQL.

ML Frontiers

ML Frontiers is an exciting competition for machine learning enthusiasts. Build, test, and deploy cutting-edge models to compete against others. Solve complex challenges and showcase your skills in predictive analytics.
