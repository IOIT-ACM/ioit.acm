Objectives

The Bit by Bit competition, hosted on HackerRank, is designed to challenge participants with algorithmic problems that test their proficiency in Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA). Compete with top minds in the field of competitive programming, hone your problem-solving abilities, and win exciting prizes. Sharpen your skills and prove your expertise in DSA!

Rules

  • Participants must register on the HackerRank platform before the competition starts to be eligible to compete.
  • Each participant will receive a set of problems to solve, focusing on Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), within a specified time frame.
  • Solutions must be submitted through the HackerRank competition platform for validation and scoring.
  • Participants may only use the programming languages supported by HackerRank for submissions.
  • Plagiarism or copying code from external sources is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate disqualification.
  • All participants are expected to adhere to HackerRank's code of conduct during the competition.
  • In case of any technical issues during the competition, participants must report them to the event coordinators immediately.
