Why Join ACM?

ACM offers exclusive access to resources, a global network, and cutting-edge opportunities for personal and professional growth. Join us to stay ahead in the world of technology, innovation, and learning.

Innovate

Access invaluable resources for lifelong learning, earn popular certifications, and stay on the cutting edge of technology.

Acquire Knowledge

Participate in insightful webinars on trending topics presented by today’s leading innovators and thought leaders.

Lifelong Learning

Gain access to the ACM Digital Library, where you can search articles, view bibliographic citations, and stay informed with the latest research.

Exclusive Discounts

Enjoy special subscription pricing on ACM Books series, journals, conferences, and much more, offering access to top-tier content.

Establish Connections

Connect with industry leaders, tech enthusiasts, and professionals across the globe, building valuable relationships and networks.

Tri-weekly Newsletter

Stay up-to-date with the latest trends, research, and industry insights through ACM's TechNews tri-weekly email digest.

ACM DSP & ESP

Participate in ACM's Distinguished Speakers Programs (DSP) and Eminent Speakers Programs (ESP) to interact with experts and thought leaders.

ACM Winter & Summer School

Get exclusive discounts on ACM’s Winter School and Summer School programs, designed to expand your technical expertise.

National Level Competitions

Represent the IOIT ACM Student Chapter at prestigious regional and national competitions, showcasing your skills on a larger stage.

Organize Events

Gain hands-on experience by organizing and participating in various technical and non-technical events hosted by the ACM chapter.

Seminars, Webinars & Lectures

Attend a range of seminars, webinars, and lectures delivered by distinguished speakers on a variety of cutting-edge topics.
