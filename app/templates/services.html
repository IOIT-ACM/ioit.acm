The Initiative

At IOIT ACM, we believe that technology should be an equalizer, not a barrier. Many non-profit organizations operate with limited digital infrastructure, which hinders their ability to scale their impact. Our student-led teams dedicate their skills to building custom software, managing data, and establishing digital presences for those who serve the community.

Efficiency

Automating manual tasks so NGOs can focus on their core mission rather than paperwork.

Reliability

Building secure, production-grade systems that are easy to maintain and scale.

Visibility

Helping organizations reach more donors and volunteers through professional digital platforms.

How We Work

01

Discovery & Consultation

We meet with your team to understand your pain points, existing workflows, and long-term goals.

02

Solution Design

Our technical leads draft a proposal including the tech stack, timeline, and resource requirements.

03

Agile Development

Student developers build the solution in sprints, providing regular updates and demos to the NGO partners.

04

Handover & Support

We deploy the solution and provide training sessions to ensure your team can use the tool effectively.
