What Happens at IOIT ACM?

IOIT ACM is the student chapter organization subsidiary of ACM. It offers students an exceptional platform to excel in both technical and non-technical domains. By participating in thought-provoking events, Special Interest Groups (SIGs), workshops, and prestigious contests, students can enhance their skills, apply their knowledge, and gain unparalleled expertise under the guidance of the IOIT ACM Student Chapter.

What is MUN?

Model United Nations (MUN) is an educational simulation where participants role-play as diplomats representing different countries. It aims to engage students in global issues and diplomatic negotiation, fostering critical thinking, public speaking, and collaboration skills. Delegates debate current events, draft resolutions, and vote on solutions to global challenges, all while striving for consensus. MUN conferences are a great opportunity for students to deepen their understanding of international relations and contribute to global discourse.

Special Interest Groups (SIGs)

The IOIT ACM Student Chapter organizes Special Interest Groups (SIGs) to foster collaboration and learning in various domains. SIGs provide a platform for students to explore, innovate, and overcome challenges together while delving into technical and non-technical areas.

Technical SIG

Focused on enhancing technical expertise, the Technical SIG conducts seminars, hands-on sessions, and discussions on projects involving cutting-edge technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, and Web Development.

  • Workshops on upcoming technologies
  • Collaborative projects
  • Problem-solving sessions

Entrepreneurship SIG

Geared towards fostering entrepreneurial skills, the Entrepreneurship SIG hosts sessions on startup building, ideation, and problem-solving in real-world scenarios. It also includes guest lectures by successful entrepreneurs.

  • Startup ideation workshops
  • Networking opportunities
  • Case studies and success stories
