People

The heart of our chapter lies in the people and students coming together to innovate, collaborate, and grow. Building a community of like-minded individuals fosters learning and long-lasting relationships.

Passion

Passion is the driving force. Our members bring an infectious enthusiasm for technology and a love for learning that fuels innovation, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the tech world.

Professionalism

Professionalism ensures quality and impact. By respecting deadlines, collaborating effectively, and communicating clearly, we ensure our initiatives meet the highest standards.

We are the largest computer science community at AISSMS IOIT

The ACM Student Chapter at AISSMS IOIT, Pune, is the largest computer science student organization in our college, fostering a community of tech enthusiasts. We welcome students of all backgrounds, interests, and skill levels to join us in sharing a passion for technology.
